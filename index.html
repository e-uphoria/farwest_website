Popular Destinations

Shuklaphanta National Park

Shuklaphanta National Park, nestled in the lush lowlands of Sudurpaschim, Nepal, is a wildlife haven known for its vast grasslands and rich biodiversity. Home to the largest herd of swamp deer in the world, the park offers a unique experience for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Badimalika

Perched atop a high-altitude hill, the Badimalika Temple offers stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas, lush meadows, and peaceful landscapes. The trek to this spiritual haven is an adventure in itself, winding through pristine forests, vibrant rhododendron blooms, and charming local villages.
